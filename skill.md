# Antigravity Skill: Answering GenAI Interview Questions

When writing explanations and answers for the AI Engineer Interview Questions repository, you MUST follow this structure and formatting to ensure consistency and high quality across all answers.

All explanations should be saved in their respective directories under `answers/<question_number>_<topic>/<topic>.md` to avoid clutter.

## Required Structure

For every answer, you must provide exactly three sections in this order:

### 1. Concept Explanation
Provide a clear, technical, and concise explanation of the concept. Break down how it works under the hood, why it matters, and any relevant technical details (like latency, context windows, architecture, etc.). Use bullet points or numbered lists where appropriate to make it easy to digest.

### 2. Real-World Example on It
Provide a concrete, real-world example of this concept in action. This could be a specific use case, a business scenario, or a practical problem being solved. Give names to hypothetical tools or companies if it helps illustrate the point (e.g., "Imagine an e-commerce chatbot built with LangChain...").

### 3. Presenting the Answer as if it Appears in the Interview
Provide a rehearsed, articulate response that the user can directly say in an interview setting. The tone should be professional, confident, and direct. It should summarize the core concept and briefly touch upon the real-world example to show practical experience. Use first-person perspective ("In my experience...", "I would approach this by...").
